Features

Content

Contributors are able to write content of their desired expertise and posted to the explore page.

Bookmark

Students can bookmark any content they like and will appear to their dashboard, so that they can easily return to them anytime.

Review

Students can review about their thoughts about the content, and also are able to leave rating to the content they read

Moderated

Don't worry, every content is moderated by our moderated users to prevent inappropriate content

Stats

Every content a contributor posts will have stats about bookmarks, reviews and average rating

about

Inspiration

Inspiration behind Scholarly is students struggling to find the study resources they need for their studies. Scholarly is trying to close that need through connecting students with contributors/tutors. This version of Scholarly is solely text-based content where contributors/tutors post content on our website, gets moderated by our moderators.
